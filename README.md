======================================
              ABOUT
  ----------------------------------

Raymond Murphy's English Grammar in Use is the world’s best-selling
grammar reference and practice book for learners of English at
intermediate (B1-B2) level. It’s perfect for self-study, but also
ideal for supplementary grammar activities in the classroom.  

The fifth edition is available as a printed book and ebook with audio,
for on-the-go learning. It comes with exercises and integrated audio
to help with listening and pronunciation skills.
